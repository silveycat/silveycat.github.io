Fiction

In my spare time I write science fiction and fantasy. My debut novel Meet Me in Another Life was published by William Morrow (US) in April 2021, and will be published by Harper Voyager (UK) in July. My author website can be found at http://catrionasilvey.com.

I'm also interested in how my former academic research topics are represented in fiction:

  • Along with Seán Roberts and Hannah Little, I am the co-organiser of Language Evolves, a workshop and short story competition aiming to introduce science fiction authors to cutting-edge research in language evolution.
  • In 2020 I participated in a panel as part of UCL's Imagining Better Futures series, discussing linguistics and science fiction in the film Arrival.
  • In 2017 I took part in a panel at WisCon on the Universal Translator trope in science fiction.
